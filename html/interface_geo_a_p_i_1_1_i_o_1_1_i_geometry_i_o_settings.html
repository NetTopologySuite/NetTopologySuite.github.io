Ordinates GeoAPI.IO.IGeometryIOSettings.AllowedOrdinates

Gets and Ordinates flag that indicate which ordinates can be handled.

This flag must always return at least Ordinates.XY.

Ordinates GeoAPI.IO.IGeometryIOSettings.HandleOrdinates

Gets and sets Ordinates flag that indicate which ordinates shall be handled.

No matter which Ordinates flag you supply, Ordinates.XY are always processed, the rest is binary and 'ed with AllowedOrdinates.
